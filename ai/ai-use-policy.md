# AI-use policy

AI tools may be used to help draft, revise, troubleshoot, and review this Quarto poster template and posters created from it.

Human users remain responsible for:

- scientific accuracy;
- authorship and acknowledgements;
- citation accuracy;
- privacy and data protection;
- permission to use logos, figures, datasets, and copyrighted material;
- final poster approval before sharing, printing, or submission.

Do not provide private, sensitive, regulated, identifiable, or restricted data to external AI tools unless the project owner has explicitly approved that workflow.

When AI tools make meaningful project-specific changes, record a short note in `ai/ai-use-log.md` summarizing what changed and what checks were run.
